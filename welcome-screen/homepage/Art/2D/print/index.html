Rupture and Reassemble

Digital Media

6*7.5 inch

It is about the disruption of the natural condition of somewhere and the following modernization upon it. The place presented here is a small town in the north-western of China. It used to have a complex configuration of the surface, including desert, mountains and canyons, and now it has become a modern town with sufficient mineral resources developed, without any left-over of its original appearance.
